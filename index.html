Many people share the dream of making movies or videos, but one individual rarely has all the resources or connections to bring that dream into reality. With crowdsourcing platforms like Kickstarter, fundraising for movie projects have become possible, but what about crowsourcing actors? cinematographers? EquiCrowd is a ongoing research project at Stanford University that aims to provide a system that enables people to collaborate on complex projects like film making online while ensuring that participants of a project is treated and rewarded fairly. What to know how we do it? Scroll down a bit to the experiment section to check out the two systems that we are testing out!
