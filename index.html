Platform

EquiCrowd is an equity-based fundraising platform for creatives

Happier Backers

Your backers get more from equity funding, like more transparency in where the money is headed. Projects also benefit from staying more engaged with backers.

Better Teams

It sucks to get a fraction of what you contributed. With equity, you can get paid what you deserve based on the success of the project.

Grow Your Investments

Early investors can profit off of projects that grow in value. This rewards those who believe in your vision early on!

How It Works

A more versatile way to crowdfund

  • Create an Equity Pool

    A project's equity represents its potential worth, such as the total donation amount for nonprofit work or expected payoff for profitable projects. Founding members divide the equity as they see fit.

  • Use Assets Like Currency

    Our platform lets you trade equity for work, or sell it for money. As your project grows in value, your equity increases in value as well.

  • Fundraise Multiple Times

    Unlike other crowdfunding platforms, our projects can raise money as often as they want, at their own discretion. It's all based on how much equity you want to sell and when.

  • Release Your Work

    When you release your work, your equity converts into money. Non-profit works divide the equity based on the total donation amount. For-profit works pay out based on the project's income.
